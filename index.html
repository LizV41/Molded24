Air Dry Clay

Creativity

Molded24 is deicided to making the best posssible air dry clay for artists for all their creative projects. Molded24 does not require any form of baking, after 12 hours the clay can no longer be re-shaped, and after 24 hours the clay will be completely dry.

24 hours later

When fully dry, Molded24 is made to be durable, but lightweight and smooth on the surface. Once fully dry, Molded 24 can be painted, sanded, or guled, making it ideal for mixed media projects.

FAQ

Does Molded24 clay require baking?
No, Molded24 is an air dry clay that dries after 24, so no need for baking, just wait it out.
What skill level is Molded24 made for?
Molded24 is made for all skill levels, useful for both beginners and professionals.
Is Molded24 non-toxic?
Yes! All Molded24 products are made to be eco-friednly, and non-toxic.
Is Molded24 good for making crafts?
Molded24 is good for making crafts, sculptures, decorative items, etc.
Can you dye Molded24 clay?
Yes, you can dye the clay using a stain, gel colorings, or chalk dust.
Does air dry clay break easily?
Air dry clay is prone to breaking, but Molded24 is built to be a durable, and will allow for small additons to your projects with no breaking.

About Us

Who we are

Molded24 was founded in Grand Rapids, Michigan in 2014 by a group of young artists. Our dream was to create a better clay, useful to artists of all skill levels. Using the best materials avaible, at a fraction of the cost of high end clay, we created a line of clay's avaible and usable for everyone.

Where We Started

Molded24 started as a small online store, in 2014 called "MoldedClay." Business began to flourish because of the unique colors and customizability we offered. By 2016 we had our first store front in Grand Rapids, Michigan. Today, we have 10 storefront locations across the north-east United States, and a online business that delivers to over 200 countries.
